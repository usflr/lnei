How to vote?

How can I vote digitally?

To vote digitally you first need to download the app. You can download it here. From there first, you need to download the ballot, then generate the ID, mark your answers, and write your randomly generated ID in the PDF ballot, finally submit it using the app or if you are having trouble use: https://www.repollo.uk.eu.org/ballot-upload.

What do I have to bring to vote?

To vote physically, you don't need to bring anything such as a pen. The organizer will have such things prepared for the elections.

We care about privacy.

At the Lardish National Electoral Institute, we believe that PRIVACY is a fundamental human right. It’s also one of our core values. Every ballot is safe and scanned by a machine or authorized representatives.

Making democracy work.

Since the 2022 Constitutional Update, the LNEI has been the highest democratic electoral authority in the country, which is in charge of the national voting processes such as local or general elections.

What are our values?

Free Democracy

The Electoral Institute is a fully democratic autonomous organization that makes sure everyone's votes are free and secret.

Pacifism

We believe in conditional pacifism and fully endorse peace talks in the event of a conflict.
