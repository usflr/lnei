2023 General Elections:


What are general elections?

According to the constitution, general elections are conducted by the LNEI to elect the members of the Lardish Senate, The House of Lardish Deputies, the President and the Prime Minister, and the Regional House of Lardish Representatives. General elections are conducted in a direct, general, free, secret, honest, and fair manner once every two years.

When are the next General Elections?

The next General Lardish Elections are scheduled to take place nationwide on

November 07th, 2023.

About the 2023 Lardish General Elections

The General Elections will take place on November 07th, 2023. Lardish citizens will be able to elect the President and Prime Minister for the 2023-2025 term, members of the Lardish Senate for a three-year-old term, members of the House of Lardish Deputies for a three-year-old term, and members of the Regional House of Lardish Representatives for a two-year-old term. 

Candidates

Lardish parties had a deadline to choose their candidates until October 01st, 2023.  The Lardish National Party, after they split from the Colish National Party, chose Lard Lapudding as their candidate for the presidency for the second term, however, they said that they wouldn't run for the Prime Minister position, instead they created a coalition with the Fritos Lardish Party, which chose Juanita Banana as the coalition's candidate for Prime Minister. On the other side, the Colish National Party (CNP) remained independent, unlike in the Colish Regional Elections. The CNP's candidates for the general election are Mrs. Yuyu (President) and Alexa Correa (Prime Minister).


Regional Elections

The Col State Republic (Great and Western) will hold regional elections on October 25th, 2023.
The expected voter turnout is 92%, which means that the citizens of the region are committed to democracy. This is just the beginning of a fully democratic era in the country. 

In this election, Colish citizens will vote for who they think is ready to take on the political duties. These include city mayors, heads of the local and regional Congress, and the governor.

The LNEI has authorized, so far, two coalitions, "United People's Cause" made up of the Colish National Party and the Lardish People's Party, and the "Free Lardish Union" formed by the Lardish National Party, the Fritos Lardish Party, and the Apple Lardish Party. Their candidates are Cutkut from the United People's Cause, and Tutita Col from the Free Lardish Union.
