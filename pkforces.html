Peacekeeping Forces

Learn about how the LNEI uses the Peacekeeping Forces to ensure stability.

Conditional Pacifism

The LNEI believes in conditional pacifism, however, if we consider it necessary (by a majority of votes) we are constitutionally authorized to use Peacekeeping Forces to neutralize any threat to the integrity of the organization.

About Peacekeepers

In 2022, Lardish Peacekeepers were designated as a division of the Lardish Forces in charge of neutralizing national conflicts such as riots to overturn an election. 

What we can and can't do

We can deploy these troops on authorized LNEI property, such as polling stations. We can't deploy these forces on private property that isn't being used by the LNEI, such as malls, houses, etc, unless it is necessary, for example:
If someone takes LNEI confidential documents, we may authorize a violent operation.
