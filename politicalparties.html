About the political parties

Currently, there are only three active political parties in the Lardish Republic. 
The Colish National Party

It is the largest party in the country, with a 70% support rate and over 2/5 of the Lardish population working at the CNP, it is a pro-monarchy party. It is considered by many an "authoritarian organization" due to its involvement in the Three Lardish War (TLW), supporting economically and militarily the Royal Lardish Republic. Recently, however, a lot of supporters left the party and switched to other parties.

The Lardish National Party

The LNP originated as a division of people from the Colish National Party who didn't agree with the "authoritarian" and pro-monarchy ideas. Like the CNP, the LNP supported militarily the Sovereign Lardish Republic in the TLW.
Lard Lapudding created this party after he split from the CNP, however, as per the law, even though the president is a member of the LNP, the de jure official ruling party is the CNP. The President, therefore, is running as the presidential candidate of the LNP.

The Lardish Fritos National Party

A recently formed party with a simple ideology, All Fritos Matter. This party originated after a Fritos massacre occurred in 2022, causing Lardish stores to dispose of over 5 tons of Fritos. Its leader, Juanita Banana, announced on August 2023 that they would run on a coalition with the LNP for the General Elections. The coalition named "Free Lardish for Everyone" chose Juanita Banana as its candidate for Prime Minister. Current polls suggest 53% support rate, which means that the FNP might need to run two times to try to win the elections.
